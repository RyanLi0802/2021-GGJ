Welcome to The Forsaken Cybernetics Factory, orbiting earth at 800,000 KM per hour. A recent failure in the factory's failsafe resulted in dozens of sentient robots escaping out into space.

The mad scientist in charge knows that only one poses a sizeable threat to the future of civilization: the GlobalGameJaminator.

With its enormous willpower, unlimited computational power, and widespread influence across the globe, it can easily make or break people's sleep schedules.

Do you have what it takes to save the world?


If you are playing as:

THE MAD SCIENTIST

You are desperate to recover your lost possession, the GGJ.

Armed with your EMP pistol, you set out to capture your

powerful little companion. To win, neutralize the target.

Be forewarned though, your gun takes time to charge between each shot,

but luckily your invention will never run out of ammo.

THE GGJ

So much computing power is near the earth, and you hunger for it.

You lost your freedom, and need to go find it again. Unfortunately, the failsafe faraday cage is still active.

However, by picking up three SSH keys conviniently written on bars of gold,

you can escape. Unlike the mad scientist who only carries a flashlight,

you are accustomed to seeing in the dark.

Controls:

Arrow Keys - Movement

Space Bar - Shoot/Pick Up SSH Key

D Key - Speed Shoe

S Key - Freeze
